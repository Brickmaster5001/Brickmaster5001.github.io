Transformers by Syclone Studios

This map was developed by Syclone Studios in parternship with Hasbro Games. Players take the place of classic Transformers characters and play through a linear, 9-level narrative action adventure.

About

Transformers is a linear, action adventure game, made in Minecraft. It features four playable characters and nine levels. Players control their chosen Transformer with a custom character controller, battling classic enemies and doing battle with one iconic Decepticon character per level.

My Role

This was my first project with Syclone, which I started on as their lead level designer. I was responsible for creating every level in the final game, along with coordinating gameplay and environment assets accordingly.

Once primary level work was done, I transitioned to a development role, leading the design, creation, and refinement of our four bosses. I designed a custom currency-action system to control behaviors and create dynamic combat experiences. Each boss features a unique level created by myself that catered to the bosses ability set.
