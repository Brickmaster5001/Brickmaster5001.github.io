Cyberpunk Underpass by Me

This map is an ongoing passion project of mine. It's an experience where players can explore, learn, and discover in a carefully crafted chunk of a wider cyberpunk world.

About

Cyberpunk Underpass is the working title of a project which I've had in development for, as of writing this, four-ish months. In that time I've finished most of the assets, have the build roughly two-thirds complete, have finished writing the backstory to the world, and have begun writing a design document to map out exactly how I want the experience to work. This means the project is close™ to reaching an MVP state. Until then, enjoy some screenshots of the build and art.
