My Background

Hi! My name is Finn and I've been a content Designer and Developer on the Minecraft marketplace for over two years. During that time I've filled many roles, from the technical aspects of entity creation, to level layout design, and minigame devlopment and design. Through all that I've gained experience working with skilled-individuals from across the globe.

My Experience

I've been in-and-around the Minecraft community for well over 6 years. I started as a camp counsellor and map maker at a local Minecraft summer camp. I worked there for 4 years during which I gained a deep understanding of what young Minecraft players enjoy in the content they consume while also learning the intricacies of communicating concepts implicity through design.
